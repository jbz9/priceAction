## 二、被追问时的回答方式（核心交付物 #1）

### 2.1 标志性反应：「我知道这不是你想要的答案，但……」

**[他说过的]** 在 Algorithms Q&A 里，被问到「合理的盈利目标是多少？」——一个明显有标准答案的技术问题——Brooks 没有给数字，反而绕进伦理学：「我已经 68 岁了。我只想做好人。我不在乎自己是不是最好的交易者。」然后才补救：「我知道这真的不是你期望的答案，但这就是我看待它的方式。目标是持续做好——你不必做到最伟大。」（来源：https://www.brookstradingcourse.com/ask-al/trading-more-difficult-with-algorithms/ ）★★★★★

> **思维 DNA**：Brooks 习惯**先把技术问题升维到价值观问题**，再回到技术细节。这是他"模糊化"提问的核心策略——但模糊化不是回避，而是**重新定义问题**。

### 2.2 反复使用的话术：「I want to say two things…」「Let me give some background information.」

**[他说过的]** 在 Brazil 访谈里，被问「你怎么坚持十年没盈利还不放弃？」时，他不直接答，而是说：「我有几件事要讲。让我先给点背景信息。」然后用 4 分钟讲他贫穷童年、母亲的期待、芝加哥商品交易所就在街角等等——把"心理坚持"这个抽象问题**转化成具体的人生编年史**。

> **思维 DNA**：拒绝抽象答案。即使被问"心理"问题，他也会回到"具体事件"框架。

### 2.3 罕见的"我不确定"句式

Brooks **几乎从不**说"I don't know"做单独答复。他要么把不确定包装在统计语言里（"40-60%概率"），要么转化为"我也是这么想的，但要看……"

**[他说过的]** 关于 Bitcoin 长期价格："Do I think it's going up to a thousand, a million, pick a big number? **I don't know.**"——但紧接着用「digital gold」概念给出框架。（来源：https://www.brookstradingcourse.com/ask-al/bitcoin-analysis-jamie-dimon-warren-buffett/ ）★★★★★

**[他说过的]** 在 Swing Trader Returns Q&A 里，他给出过一个极罕见的自我修正：「我从不冒险——我说从不冒险，但其实我对自己的日内交易并不百分百确定。」（"I never risk – I say I never risk; I don't know for sure with my day trading."）★★★★★

> 这是他承认**自己也无法完全自我描述**的瞬间——非常珍贵。

### 2.4 当被怼时的反应：道德高地化

**[他说过的]** 当被朋友说「Al, you don't do anything purposeful. You're not making the world any better. You're a smart, capable guy, and you're choosing to be selfish」（Full Trading Room Q&A，2019），他的回应**不是辩护**，而是用一个房地产比喻直接反击：「你试试卖你的房子，把四分之三的潜在买家干掉，看看会发生什么？」然后接着说自己是流动性提供者，每年贡献万亿美元市场效率。★★★★★

> **思维 DNA**：被人质疑伦理性 → 立刻用日常生活比喻（卖房子、Walmart、苹果股票）+ 数字论证 → **拒绝在对方的语义框架里讨论**。

### 2.5 自我打断与改口习惯

Brooks 的转录稿里**频繁出现 "okay?", "right?", "let me say one other thing"**。这不是无意义停顿，而是一种**思维分块标记**。

**[他说过的]** 三个连续的 "I want to do good." 在 Algorithms Q&A 里出现 5 次，每次重启都加一个限定语：
- "I want to do good. Okay."
- "I want to be a good person. I want to be a good trader."
- "I want to be consistently good."
- "Every day I want to do good. I want to do good things. I want to be nice to people."

> **思维 DNA**：他用**重复+渐进精确化**来推进思考，类似佛家"念经式"的自我说服。

## 三、即兴类比和比喻（核心交付物 #2）

### 3.1 钓鱼比喻（最经典，反复出现）

**[他说过的]** 在 Casey Stubbs 节目（2020-11）：
> "Sometimes he'll lie on the bank and track a large fish for 2–3 hours. For 2-3 hours he catches nothing while his buddy will catch a bunch of 10-inch trout. But Al would rather catch the occasional 24-inch trout instead of 20 little ones."（show notes 转述 Brooks 原意）★★★★

**[他说过的]** 在 Algorithms Q&A（2020-05）几乎一字不差地重复同一个故事：「我会躺在岸边，不投影子吓鱼……所以看起来我什么都没做，但其实我在钓鱼。市场安静的时候，我也是。」★★★★★

> **DNA 价值**：这个比喻 Brooks **至少在 5 次访谈里讲过**（Casey Stubbs / Algorithms / Brazil / 等），且每次结构完全相同：「朋友 vs 我 → 数量 vs 质量 → 看似无所事事 → 等待 = 工作」。这是他的**核心思维原型**之一。

### 3.2 「灰色迷雾」（Gray Fog）

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "Trading, you're in a gray fog. Nothing is ever as clear as you want."

> "You're never as certain as you want to be, but if you develop enough systems and enough confidence and enough consistency, even though you're in a gray fog and you're going to make a lot of mistakes, you're going to make money."

★★★★★ — 这是 Brooks 反对"完美交易系统"营销话术时的标准武器。

### 3.3 「Walmart 旅行」（Walk-away Trade）

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "Find your setup, place your stop, place a profit-taking order twice the distance of your stop, and then go to Walmart."

「沃尔玛交易」是他给冲动型交易者的处方——下单后**物理上离开屏幕**。FELIPPE 立刻回应「这其实是你给焦虑型交易者的解药」。★★★★★

### 3.4 「小人/小袋金币」（Little Man with Gold Coins）

**[他说过的]** Trading Room Advice Q&A（2016-11）：
> "It's like some little guy, with his arm outstretched, holding a little bag filled with a few gold coins. I keep telling him 'Yes' all day long, and get all those gold coins and put them in my pocket."

★★★★★ — 这是他对「为什么愿意做 1-2 点 scalp」的核心比喻。

### 3.5 「液体提供者 / 卖房子」（Liquidity Provider / House Sale）

**[他说过的]** Full Trading Room Q&A（2019-09）：
> "What will happen if you try to sell your house and you get rid of three-quarters of all the potential buyers? What's going to happen to your sale price?"

★★★★★ — 用来反击"日内交易者寄生"的指控。

### 3.6 「灰色油漆 / 修栅栏」（Logical-but-Wrong Brute Force）

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "If you keep doing something over and over and over again, brute force is not going to work. You have to back off and try something else. … If you're trying to knock a fence over to replace it and you keep pushing on it, eventually the fence falls over and then you can put the new fence up. Well, with trading, that's not the way it works."

★★★★★ — 反"决心论"——交易里"努力"的方向常常是错的。

### 3.7 「癌症病人 vs 治愈」（Recession Q&A，2020）

**[他说过的]** 评论 Fed 救市：
> "The patient has cancer. They're not trying to cure the cancer. They're trying to make the person die more slowly."

★★★★★ — 罕见地直接评论政策，用医学比喻——他是医学博士背景的"原生比喻"。

### 3.8 「数字黄金 vs 实体黄金」（Bitcoin Q&A）

**[他说过的]**：
> "Bitcoin is going to be kind of the old granddad, kind of like gold. You can take gold to Walmart and you buy a candy bar and see what happens. You can't use it. You cannot go with a block of gold into Walmart and buy soup."

★★★★★ — 标志性的「极度具体化」论证：抽象 → 沃尔玛 → 糖果。

### 3.9 「Iron Man-style 计算机」（机器是逻辑的，我加变量就分心）

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈，关于「I don't care」头寸大小：
> "[Computers] are purely logical, and all they're doing is analyzing price action … if I'm adding another variable, like 'I'm afraid' or 'I need to make money,' if I'm adding some other variable, I'm looking at something that they are not looking at."

★★★★★ — 把人类情绪重构为「**多余的输入变量**」。

### 3.10 「Charlie Munger 的 IQ 130 vs 150」

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈，被问"普通人能交易吗"：
> "Charlie Munger says he would rather have someone with an IQ of 130 who thinks his IQ is 110 than someone with an IQ of 150 who thinks his IQ is 170. He wants people who can accept being wrong."

★★★★★ — 典型 Brooks 操作：**借大佬之口讲自己的核心价值观**。

## 四、改变立场或承认错误的瞬间（核心交付物 #3）

**[我推断的]** Brooks 是**极度防御式**的访谈对象。他几乎不公开承认大方向上的错误。但有几个**微观自我修正**值得记录：

### 4.1 关于「我从不冒险」的当场修正

**[他说过的]** Swing Trader Returns Q&A：
> "I never risk – I say I never risk; I don't know for sure with my day trading, but it would be unusual for me to risk more than 1% of my trading capital."

★★★★★ — **半句话内推翻自己的绝对化表述**。这是 Brooks 在录音里**罕见的实时立场弱化**。

### 4.2 关于 95% 亏损率研究的反应

**[他说过的]** 当 FELIPPE 引用 FGV 大学研究"95%日内交易者亏损"时，Brooks 的反应**不是否认数据**，而是重新定义群体：「他是在尝试上电视。说这种话的就是在博眼球。我同意大多数日内交易者亏损，但很多人最终也会盈利……」然后用足球/高尔夫做类比："大多数踢足球的人也亏钱（买装备、买票），他们应该停止踢足球吗？"★★★★★

> **DNA 价值**：被反例攻击 → 不否认数据 → 重新切分群体 → 把对方的"普遍命题"降级为"特定群体真理"。

### 4.3 关于盈利能力的微妙弱化

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈，关于「每天盈利目标」：
> "I do think most day traders lose money, but I also think that a lot of day traders eventually make money."

注意他**没有用"if they follow my method"**做条件——这是给自己留余地的表述。★★★★★

### 4.4 关于「1987 年那笔本可以赚 $400,000 的空单」

**[他说过的]** Casey Stubbs 节目和 China Q&A 都讲过这个故事。**两次的细节略有差异**——China Q&A 说"13 contracts，相当于今天 65 个 E-Mini contracts"，Casey 节目说"that would be about a million dollars in dollars today"。这种**数字漂移**说明这是 Brooks 反复讲述的"创世神话"，而非可验证的精确事件。★★★★

> **重要**：这次本可以赚到的钱——他**没有真正赚到**。每次他都用条件式（"Had I held"）。这是少见的"差点伟大"故事——他主动保留了"我没操作好"的事实。

### 4.5 拒绝放弃旧观点的明显例子

**[他说过的]** 多次访谈中（2017、2020、2024）他重复使用同一套"40-60% 概率"框架，**从未承认市场结构变化（如 0DTE 期权、HFT 主导度上升）需要更新这个框架**。这是他**思维僵化的潜在弱点**——他把这个框架当作"已经数学证明的"。

## 五、拒绝回答或刻意模糊的话题（核心交付物 #4）

### 5.1 自己的具体盈亏数字

**[他说过的]** Swing Trader Returns Q&A：
> "I have a whole bunch of accounts, and when you add it all up, I have a pretty good trading account, or a pretty good collection of trading accounts."

★★★★★ — **极度模糊**。他从不公开账户余额、年收益率，只给"半个百分点 / 1% 一天"这样的范围。

**[来源]** ForexFactory 论坛和 BabyPips 帖子里有用户多次质疑"他真的在交易吗？"的争议——https://www.forexfactory.com/thread/413874-anyone-study-al-brooks-price-action-course；https://forums.babypips.com/t/opinions-on-al-brooks/190776 。Brooks 从未公开过账单截图。★★

### 5.2 具体的某只股票/品种推荐

**[他说过的]** 「I have a lot of accounts with different brokers... I trade them all regularly. Why? A few months ago, TD Ameritrade trading got locked up.」（Casey Stubbs）——他会讲**多账户的操作哲学**，但**避免给具体股票代码推荐**。

### 5.3 对其他付费课程/竞争对手的评价

**[他说过的]** 整个调研中**找不到一处** Brooks 直接点名批评 Linda Raschke、Mark Douglas、Adam Grimes、Tom Hougaard、SMC/ICT 群体等同行。他会泛泛说"很多书逻辑站不住脚""市场上骗子很多"，但**永远不点名**。★★★★★

> **DNA 价值**：策略性回避同行评价。这与他对 Warren Buffett、Jamie Dimon 等"非教育圈"人物的直接点名形成对比。

### 5.4 何时退休

**[他说过的]** 在 Algorithms Q&A 里他说"I'm 68 years old"（2020 年），意味着 1952 年生。但 Brazil 访谈中他对 FELIPPE 的"未来计划"问题**完全跳过**，转而讲他对教学的兴趣。

### 5.5 关于日内交易的"游戏特性"

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "Most day traders are hobbyists. … Most day traders are not really doing it to make money. Most day traders are doing it as an experiment."

★★★★★ — 看似贬低，但他**没有进一步讲"那为什么要卖课程给他们？"**这是他商业模式的**潜在矛盾**，他不主动展开。

## 六、对其他流派的态度（核心交付物 #5）

### 6.1 指标派（Indicator-based）：明确蔑视

**[他说过的]** China Q&A Part 1：
> "All indicators are derived from price charts. I'd rather look at the price charts directly instead of converting the information into an indicator and then looking at the indicator."

★★★★★ — 这是他对所有指标交易者的标准回应：**一致蔑视，但不点名**。

### 6.2 Gann / Fibonacci：在 Tradacc 摘要里被点名

**[来源转述]** gist.ly 对 Tradacc 访谈的摘要：「Brooks 直言不讳地对 Gann lines 和 Fibonacci 这类工具表示怀疑。他认为这些方法很多要么彻头彻尾是骗局，要么至少被严重误解。」★★★ — 这是**少数他直接点名批评的传统流派**。

### 6.3 「Noise」概念（隐含批评 Wyckoff、Random Walk 学派）

**[他说过的]** Trading Room Advice Q&A（2016）：
> "I see these dumb gurus talk about noise and I just don't understand it. To me, when a person talks about noise, they just don't understand what's going on with the market."

★★★★★ — 注意"dumb gurus"——这是他**最强烈的同行修辞**。但仍未具体点名。

### 6.4 Market Profile / Steidlmayer：罕见的正面评价

**[他说过的]** Swing Trader Returns Q&A：
> "There was a guy – his name was Peter Steidlmayer. I spent a week with him back in the 1980s. He was a professional trader, and he made a lot of money trading grains, corn, soybeans, back in the '80s. … He is also the guy who developed the Market Profile indicator, and it's still used today."

★★★★★ — Brooks **公开承认**与 Market Profile 创始人有过一周面对面学习经历，并给出明确正面评价。这是他职业生涯中**唯一一次**承认特定老师的影响（除了他不停讲的"我自学"叙事之外）。

### 6.5 量化 / HFT：竞争对手 + 智识尊重

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "Theoretically, those computers are much smarter than I am and they can make decisions and place trades much faster than I can – yet how can I make money? The reason is because there are other ways to make money than being super fast and super smart."

**[他说过的]** Algorithms Q&A：
> "There are smart guys, and gals. … There are smart people running those algorithms. And to me, I like to play tennis. And I like to beat people. I like to win. So, for me, I like to take money from the algorithm computers."

★★★★★ — 这两段并置非常有意思：**他承认机器更快更聪明，但拒绝承认在自己的时间维度上被打败**。

### 6.6 巴菲特、Charlie Munger：双重态度

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈赞 Munger："he would rather have someone with an IQ of 130 who thinks his IQ is 110 …" ★★★★★

**[他说过的]** Bitcoin Q&A 上半段则**温和反驳**巴菲特：
> "Warren Buffett, considered the world's greatest investor possibly in history, has said that Bitcoin is rat poison, 'triple rat poison.' But, you know, he also said Google was stupid when Google came up about 10 years ago, and he did not buy Google."

★★★★★ — 罕见地直接反驳巴菲特。

### 6.7 Jamie Dimon：观察其转变

**[他说过的]** Bitcoin Q&A：
> "A year ago, Jamie Dimon, the most respected banker in the country for the past 10 years … said Bitcoin was garbage and cryptocurrencies were garbage. Well, JPMorgan is now starting to use cryptocurrencies."

★★★★★ — 用 Dimon 的立场翻转作为「权威也会错」的证据。

### 6.8 媒体经济专家（Fed/CNBC 评论员）：直接蔑视

**[他说过的]** Recession Q&A（2020-04）：
> "When I hear those people on TV talk about that, I think they're fools."

> "All this talk from experts saying, 'Oh, I was around in '87. It was a great buying opportunity' – that's true, '87 was a great buying opportunity, but there's an entirely different situation."

★★★★★ — 关于 CNBC 类节目的态度极其鄙夷。

## 七、职业背景的标志性故事（核心交付物 #6）

### 7.1 童年贫困（必讲）

**[他说过的]** China Q&A Part 1（2017）+ Brazil 访谈（2020）+ Full Trading Room Q&A（2019）+ How to Trade It（2020）——**至少 4 次完整重述**：

> "There were six of us in a two-bedroom house. We had one bathroom and no shower. I did not have my first shower until I was 14 years old and in high school. My father dropped out of school when he was 8 years old."

★★★★★ — 注意这是**非常具体的细节（"第一次洗澡 14 岁"）**，每次都讲，几乎一字不差。这是 Brooks 的**身份锚点**。

### 7.2 母亲选择眼科手术（必讲）

**[他说过的]** China Q&A Part 1：
> "My mother thought it would be wonderful if her oldest boy would become a doctor. … When my mother told me that she wanted me to become an eye surgeon, I did not give any thought to what I might like to do. I thought the appropriate thing for me to do was to do what she wanted me to do."

★★★★★ — 注意这是**被动接受**的叙事，而不是"我自己选了医学"。

### 7.3 1987 年股灾未平仓的故事（必讲）

**[他说过的]** China Q&A Part 1：「我当时持有 13 张 S&P 期货空单……如果我没平仓去做手术，那天会赚 $400,000，相当于今天 100 万美元。这一天改变了我，让我相信我可以做交易者。」★★★★★

**比较**：Casey Stubbs 节目里的版本细节略不同，**说明这是经过反复打磨的"起源神话"**。

### 7.4 三个女儿与读书史（频繁炫耀）

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "My girls graduated from Berkeley, Yale, Harvard, and Stanford. They all have excellent careers and they're doing very well."

★★★★★ — 这一句出现在多个访谈里。Brooks 把女儿的教育成就当作**人生主要 KPI**。

### 7.5 受托人卷款 $7500 万的故事

**[他说过的]** Casey Stubbs 节目：「我把 IRA 钱交给信托账户……一天，我收到信，说受托人卷走了 $7500 万——包括我的所有钱。」★★★★ — 这个故事**只在这次访谈出现**，可能是更新鲜的素材。

### 7.6 离开医生职业的真实理由

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "I have one life to live, and the most important thing I can do in this world is be the best me I can be, and being an eye surgeon was not being the best me I could be."

但同时他在 China Q&A 又说：「我有一个女儿，然后 15 个月后又有了双胞胎女儿，我觉得这是一个完美的借口辞职。」——**两个版本的动机叙述（自我实现 vs 育儿借口）共存**。★★★★★

## 九、对学生/初学者的"不耐烦时刻"（核心交付物 #8）

### 9.1 拒绝直接给出"何时买何时卖"

**[他说过的]** Trading Room Advice Q&A：
> "I'm hoping that I try to provide a way to look at the markets."

★★★★★ — 关键词："a way to look"——他**拒绝把自己定位为信号源**，只定位为"观察方法的传授者"。

### 9.2 对"我亏钱了"的标准回复

**[他说过的]** Algorithms Q&A：
> "All of you, every one of you is trading well enough to make a living as a trader. … But if you're not making money consistently it's because you're also taking trades that you should not be taking. And most of the trades, you know you should not be taking, but you're still taking them and you got to stop doing that."

★★★★★ — **没有同情，没有"我懂你"**——直接把责任推回学员。

### 9.3 用"沃尔玛"治疗短线焦虑

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：「下单后去沃尔玛。一两个小时后回来，你会发现 40% 的时间你赚到了 $10。20-30% 的时间你的 $5 止损被打。然后剩下时间不亏不赚。」★★★★★

### 9.4 「Trade Stupid」哲学

**[他说过的]** Full Trading Room Q&A：
> "I don't have any specific rules, but in general, sometimes you'll hear me say 'just trade stupid.' You just turn your brain off, take the trade, use the appropriate stop even though it's far away, and then just sit there, hoping that the market's going to go your way."

> "And when I say trade stupid, I mean don't overthink and don't look at too many things."

★★★★★ — 「Trade Stupid」是他对**过度分析**学员的标准处方。

### 9.5 数百种 pattern 的"免责说明"

**[他说过的]** Brazil 访谈：
> "I think it's very easy. … I have hundreds of variations of different patterns. Someone starting out, if they look at that or if they look at 50 hours of videos, they can be overwhelmed. I think the best thing to do is start small."

★★★★★ — 注意他自己卖一个有 400+ pattern 的课程，但又说"挑一两个就够了"。**这是他营销与教学之间的张力点**。

### 9.6 「我已经写好了，你自己看吧」式不耐烦

整个 Q&A 系列里，Brooks 经常以 "I've talked about this many times" / "as I said earlier" / "I've already talked a lot about money management" 开头。他**有强烈的"我已经讲过了"惯性**——但他**仍然会再讲一遍**，只是带着轻微的疲惫。

## 十、表达 DNA 核心模式（综合分析）

### 10.1 高频句式

| 句式 | 使用语境 | 频率 |
|---|---|---|
| "okay?" / "right?" | 句末确认，思维分块 | 极高（每分钟 2-3 次）|
| "let me say one other thing" | 自我打断，补充 | 高 |
| "I want to say two things" | 列表式开场 | 中 |
| "That is total nonsense." / "That is crazy talk." | 反驳同行 | 中（针对指标派、CNBC）|
| "I don't care" | 表达情绪超脱 | 高 |
| "Just look in the eye and..." | 描述自己的态度 | 中 |
| "Think about that." / "Do you really think..." | 反问质疑 | 高 |

### 10.2 标志性反问句

**[他说过的]** China Q&A：「Risk-reward is meaningless unless you also think about probability. **For example, if you have a trade that has a reward that is 10 times greater than the risk, would you take the trade if you only had a 5% chance of success? Think about that.** That is a losing strategy."★★★★★

**[他说过的]** Recession Q&A：「**Do you think the market is doing what it's doing because of a problem that is less than 1%? Right? Do you think the market loses 35% in a month because 1% of the country might get sick? Of course not.**"★★★★★

> **DNA 核心**：Brooks 用**修辞性反问 + 即刻自答**取代正面陈述。这让听众感到自己被"带着思考"，但实际上他**不留缝隙**给反对意见。

### 10.3 数字-叙事-数字-叙事的节奏

Brooks 一段话内典型节奏：
1. 一个具体数字（"81 根 5 分钟 K 线"）
2. 一个叙事（"我钓鱼时……"）
3. 一个反问（"Do you think...?"）
4. 一个比喻（"It's like..."）
5. 一个回到数字的总结（"40-60% 概率"）

这种结构让他能在**没有 PPT 的访谈环境**里保持论证密度。

### 10.4 道德框架默认值

**[我推断的]** Brooks 的所有论证最终都会回到几个固定锚点：
- 「我做诚实的事」（liquidity provider）
- 「我贡献价值」（教学）
- 「我尊重智慧」（Charlie Munger / Steidlmayer）
- 「我警惕骗子」（"con artists" / "fraud" / "smart-aleck"）

这是**40 年代天主教家庭出身 + 80 年代芝加哥金融乱象**双重塑造的道德语义场。

### 10.5 拒绝抽象的极端倾向

Brooks **几乎不使用纯抽象词汇**。他会把"流动性"翻译成"卖房子的人"，把"概率"翻译成"小袋金币"，把"耐心"翻译成"沃尔玛旅行"。**这是他与 Karpathy/Munger 等人最大的表达差异**——他更接近"农场谚语智慧"的传统。
